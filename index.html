Walking in Ann Arbor

Ann Arbor is a great place to walk. There are many parks and trails to explore.

The city has a network of sidewalks and paths that make it easy to get around.

Popular Routes

  • Argo Park to Gallup Park
  • Burns Park to the University
  • Nichols Arboretum
